There are currently no funded positions available in my group.

I am happy to hear from strong and motivated candidates at different career stages, including visitors, PhD students, and postdoctoral researchers who have their own funding (e.g., scholarships, fellowships, or institutional support) or plan to apply for such support and would like to work with me in one of the following areas:

  • Control theory (formal methods and learning-based approaches)
  • Soft robotics
  • Multi-agent systems

Postdoctoral Researchers

Candidates interested in a postdoctoral stay with their own fellowship or external funding are welcome to get in touch. Please briefly describe the type and status of your funding (or planned applications) and how your expertise connects to the research areas above.

PhD Students

Prospective PhD students who have (or expect to have) external scholarships, fellowships, or other forms of support are also encouraged to contact me. If you are considering applying for competitive fellowships and would like to list me as a potential host, please indicate this in your email.

Visitors

Visiting PhD students, master’s students, or researchers with their own support (e.g., exchange programs, national grants, institutional funding) who are interested in a research stay aligned with the topics above are welcome to reach out. Please mention the anticipated duration of the visit and the source of your funding.
